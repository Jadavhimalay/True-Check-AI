Detailed Breakdown

Here is the complete end-to-end workflow used by TrueCheck AI:

  1. Submission: You paste a URL or article text. Metadata is extracted.
  2. Claim Extraction: AI identifies factual claims, entities, and key details.
  3. Source Reputation Check: Publisher is compared with credible databases.
  4. Cross-Referencing: Claims checked against news APIs and official sources.
  5. Context Review: Timeline, outdated references, and reused assets are examined.
  6. Scoring: Transparent confidence score based on evidence strength.
  7. Human Review (Optional): High-risk claims may be escalated.
  8. Results: Clean verdict + evidence breakdown.
